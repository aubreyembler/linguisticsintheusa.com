Linguistics In The USA Inc.

Formed for the primary purpose of developing and disseminating a body of new knowledge relating to the social sciences, the primary activity of Linguistics In The USA Inc. consist of the performance of scientific research under contracts with governmental agencies, the results being communicated to the public through seminar courses, lectures, and public discussions, and through publications distributed free to depositary libraries.
